Energy Categories Explained:

  • Divine Transcendence - The highest state of pure consciousness beyond form
  • Sacred Serenity - Divine bliss and complete peace
  • Ecstatic Harmony - Joyful unity with all existence
  • Unconditional Devotion - Boundless love without attachment
  • Perfect Understanding - Complete clarity and wisdom
  • Complete Forgiveness - Total acceptance of what is
  • Optimistic Surrender - Willing participation in life
  • Calm Detachment - Peaceful neutrality
  • Empowered Affirmation - The foundation of positive change
  • Scornful Superiority - False pride and judgment
  • Hateful Aggression - Destructive anger
  • Craving Enslavement - Addiction and wanting
  • Anxious Withdrawal - Protective fear
  • Regretful Sadness - Loss and disappointment
  • Hopeless Despair - Giving up on life
  • Self-blame Punishment - Internal condemnation
  • Miserable Existence - The lowest state of consciousness
